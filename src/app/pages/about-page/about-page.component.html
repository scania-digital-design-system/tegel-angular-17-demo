About this page

Testing ground for the 1.53.0-icons-beta.0 beta of @scania/tegel-angular-17. The sections below cover the components affected by the variable-migration work: Link, Breadcrumbs, Divider, Footer, Button, Tooltip and Tag, across multiple states and both brands. A final section renders every icon name from the union of ScaniaIcons and TratonIcons so the rendered glyph for each can be visually verified.
